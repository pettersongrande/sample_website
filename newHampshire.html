Ski The East Guide

Epic Mountains

New Hampshire

  • Crotched Mountain

    • GO BOLDLY Crotched Mountain is out-of-this-world day or night. From first tracks with 1000' of vertical to shredding under the stars with night skiing and riding, we've got enough adventure to keep you out all day and night. Home to the Rocket high speed quad and Midnight Madness, there's no place on Earth like Crotched.

  • Mount Sunapee

    • Where Family Traditions Take Flight For 75 years, we've been proud to be your destination for family fun in Southern New Hampshire. Celebrate generations of traditions and make a few new ones with your loved ones at Sunapee this season.

  • WildCat Mountain

    • STEP INTO THE WILD Wildcat Mountain, tucked in the legendary Pinkham Notch, is consistently ranked the most scenic mountain in the East. With unbeatable views and famous terrain, Wildcat will get you in touch with your wild side.

  • Attitash Mountain

    • ADVENTURE IN THE HEART OF THE WHITE MOUNTAINS Two mountain peaks offering over 300 acres of endless adventure. Discover something for everyone around every turn from classic New England-style skiing on Attitash to wide-open cruisers on Bear Peak.
